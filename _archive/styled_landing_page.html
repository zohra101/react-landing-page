Private session details

Private sessions allow yogis to give individuals more time than in a group class, so we can provide deeper instruction, customized practices to fit your goals and needs, additional variations for poses, and new ways to use props. Dubbed the queen of props during her yoga teacher training, Alex is experienced with the following standard yoga props--blankets, blocks, bolsters, and straps--as well as a few uncommon props. Please contact us for more information aboout props.

Or we can guide you through mediation or savasna for the whole session if that's what your body needs that day. Meet yourself where you are!

Sessions last 30, 45, or 60 minutes.
